On a tiny ship with excellent amenities, cruise the gorgeous Oslofjord and take in the views of two of Scandinavia's most popular towns.

Go on a springtime cruise and explore two stunning destinations in Scandinavia. Explore the attractions of Gothenburg and Oslo while sailing aboard the tiny ship Balmoral, which has two pools, six dining options, seven bars, and wide open decks to enjoy the scenery.

Traveling along Oslofjord, a charming body of water surrounded by thick forests and dotted with charming islands and enticing beaches where inhabitants congregate to unwind, is the first leg of your trip.

Discover Oslo's museums and cultural landmarks, including the Viking burial grounds, the Vigeland Sculpture Park, the Munch Museum, and the Holmenkollen Ski Museum.

Visit vibrant Trädgårdsföreningen, one of Europe's best-kept 19th-century parks, from the dock in central Gothenburg. There, you can explore forests, a rose garden, and a tropical palm house.


You'll have two complete days at sea to enjoy life on board Balmoral, with its excellent facilities and varied evening entertainment.

Enjoy this luxurious trip to see the wonders of Rotterdam and Amsterdam as well as the stunning Dutch countryside.

Take a wonderful voyage through the Netherlands in the spring, when the country's flower fields are at their most picturesque.

Adam Frost, the renowned gardener and broadcaster, will go on a portion of this trip with you. He'll lead you on a visit to Keukenhof Gardens, where you'll witness an astounding seven million blooms spread across 80 acres in a kaleidoscope of color, as well as a special discussion and Q&A.

A visit to Kinderdijk, where 19 windmills line the banks of the canal and serene marshes surround them, is another highlight of the journey

Combine tours of the ancient villages with time spent in the vibrant cities of Rotterdam, where you can take in the striking architectural contrasts between the old harbor and the new metropolis, and Amsterdam, with its museums, canals, and fashionable nightlife.


While During this trip you'll stay on the luxurious MS Emily Brontë. All meals are included in the elegant restaurant or the sleek Riverview Kitchen, where you can take in the views from the ship's panoramic windows.

Together with Tom Kerridge, navigate the Venetian Lagoon's islands.

This area of northwest Italy is home to breathtaking architecture, renowned art museums, gorgeous islands, and a vibrant past. The best way to see everything the Lagoon has to offer is to take a luxurious boat around Venice's famous canals.

During your eight-day voyage, the opulent S.S. La Venezia—a tiny ship with exquisite furnishings inspired by the elegant elegance of Venice—will serve as your home away from home. Michelin-starred chef Tom Kerridge will make unique appearances on this voyage, including a private Q&A about his life and work, an onboard discussion, and a cocktail event with picture opportunities. Along with tiny sample dishes, Tom will also provide a culinary demonstration.

VIP access to the Doge's palace for an early-morning tour is also included in the package. There are plenty additional included trips as well. There are plenty of activities to keep you busy, including cycling the beaches of Chioggia or taking walking tours to discover more about the lagoon's history.

There's plenty of time to see eveything this stunning island has to offer and to pay visits to Burano, Mazzorbo, Torcello, Chioggia, and Murano, giving you a complete picture of life in the Lagoon


And, when you're not busy exploring on land, enjoy relaxing with the wonderful facilities on S.S. La Venezia, including three dining venues, a spa, library, lounge and bar.

Explore Scotland's breathtaking Highlands and islands on a luxurious tiny ship

Enjoy a lovely cruise while taking in the breathtaking views of Scotland's islands and Highlands. You will see some of the most stunning places in this region of the globe on this amazing luxury vacation, including secluded sea lochs and the charming isles off the western coast of Scotland.

You'll go to well-known locations including Loch Ness, Oban, Mull, and Iona, which are home to a variety of interesting local plants and animals. Additionally, you will cross the well-known Caledonian Canal. The exquisite Lord of the Glens, a charming little ship resembling the Royal Yacht Britannia, is your vessel for your excursion. You can count on a secluded and tranquil stay with just 26 outward-facing ensuite staterooms and a maximum of 54 guests

Go with Monica Galetti to Bordeaux and the Medoc vineyards

Bordeaux is one of the most beautiful areas in France. Take advantage of discounts of £1,300 per person (capacity regulated) on this specially designed tour around the Dordogne, Garonne, and Gironde to visit the picturesque landscapes and notable cultural sites.

During a portion of the tour, renowned chef Monica Galetti will accompany you. She will take you to a farmer's market in Libourne where she will conduct a live culinary demonstration and conduct a Q&A

From Blaye to Bourg, you'll drive the Route de la Corniche Fleurie, passing by the imposing Blaye Fortress and the Lansac Windmill. Enjoy a number of wine tastings, village excursions, a visit to the farmers' market, and a walking tour of Bordeaux's historical legacy.

This week, you'll be based out of the S.S. Bon Voyage, a graceful vessel whose interior design draws inspiration from the grace of France. On board, there are several excellent dining options, including Le Grand Fromage's locally-inspired meals and La Brasserie's bistro food.
